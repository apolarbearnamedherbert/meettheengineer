Meet the Engineer

"I like to make things."


About

"Hey look, buddy. I'm an engineer. That means I solve problems.
Not problems like "What is beauty?" 'cause that would fall within the purview of your conundrums of philosophy.
I solve practical problems."
- The Engineer, on the nature of engineering

The Engineer is a fine Southern gentleman from Texas who is as dangerous as he is amiable. Don't be fooled by his friendly smile or his soothing guitar: the man is a stone-cold killer. His area of expertise is building machines that support his teammates. The result? Complete area denial.


Abilities

  • Gun

    • The Engineer ain't one to showboat, but if he needs to defend himself, he most definitely can.

  • More Gun

    • The Engineer builds a sentry gun to defend an area. This gun automatically detects and shoots at targets and can be leveled up for more firepower.

  • Drugstore Cowboy

    • The Engineer builds a dispenser to support teammates. The dispenser gives health and ammo to any teammate who stands within its radius. It can be upgraded to offer supplies much more quickly.

  • Battle Rustler

    • The Engineer builds a set of teleporters to get teammates to the front line much more quickly. Players can enter the teleporter entrance and immediately be taken to the location of the teleporter exit. This building can be upgraded to send players to the front lines more quickly.


In the Comics

Just a short king sipping some coffee.

The Engineer's plotline in the main set of TF2 comics is quite separate from that of his former coworkers. After all of the mercs are fired, Engineer is hired by the Administrator to maintain her life-extending machine. Because the Administrator is the Administrator, shenanigans are implied to ensue in the 7th comic. Don't worry, though. You don't have to be afraid. The 7th comic is never coming out, after all.

The Engineer is also featured in the short comics Loose Canon, True Meaning, Death of a Sales-Bot, Gargoyles and Gravel, and Shadow Boxers.
